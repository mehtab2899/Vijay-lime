our products

hydrated lime

Hydrated Lime, is a dry fine , white powder with chemical formula Ca(OH)2. The Hydrated Lime manufactured in our state-of-art plant is free flowing fine powder, without any grits and impurities.

quick lime

We manufacturers highly reactive and high purity quick lime lumps required by many industrial processes. We manufacture quicklime lumps from sorted supreme quality limestone of our own mines.

Reactive Quick Lime

Quick Lime Lumps, also known as ' Burnt Lime' or ' Calcined Lime' are formed by calcining Calcium Carbonate ( Lime Stone). The Products procesed using best quality chemical and offer flawless supply at the users end.

Quick Lime Powder

Quick Lime Powder offered by us is rigorously checked on various parameters by quality experts and packed with high precision so as to meet the industry quality standards and also provide best quality.
